Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Harmonique

General

What is Harmonique?

Harmonique is a local music player designed to provide a seamless and personalized listening experience. It allows you to play songs stored on your device with intuitive controls and customizable settings.

Is Harmonique free to use?

Yes! Harmonique is completely free and open-source. You can download and use it without any subscriptions or hidden fees.

Account & Settings

How do I change my language preferences?

Go to Settings > Language and select your preferred language. Changes will take effect after restarting the app.

Can I disable explicit content?

Yes! Under Settings > Explicit Content, toggle the option to skip explicit music and podcasts. Note that it may take a moment for the changes to apply.

What does Autoplay do?

Autoplay ensures nonstop listening by queuing similar songs when your current track ends. You can enable or disable this feature in Settings > Autoplay.

Troubleshooting

Why can't I see my music library?

Ensure the app has permission to access your device's storage. If the issue persists, try restarting the app or re-importing your music files.

The app crashes when I open it. What should I do?

Try clearing the app cache or reinstalling Harmonique. If the problem continues, contact our support team with details about your device and OS version.

Support

How can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

You can submit feedback directly through the app or visit our GitHub repository to open an issue. We appreciate your input!

Where can I download the latest version?

Check the official Harmonique website or GitHub page for updates and release notes.
